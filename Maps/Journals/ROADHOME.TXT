Map:  The Road Home

The sorceress Faelina and her twin, the warrior Faelira, are defenders of a forest realm.  The woodland peace is shattered by an invasion of evil.  Now, Faelira must hold off the invaders with what forces she can muster, while Faelina seeks a safe path home through enemy lines to reach Evenstar, where an army awaits that can defeat even the powerful invaders who are trying to destroy her beloved homeland.
